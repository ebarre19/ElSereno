Geography of El Sereno


El Sereno is 4.17 square miles and has a population of 43,766. It has a population density of 9,826 people per square mile.The neighboring communities consist of Alhambra, Boyle Heights, East Los Angeles, Highland Park, Lincoln Heights, Montecito Heights, Monterey Park and South Pasadena.

There are two sub-division areas within El Sereno:

  • Hill Side Vilage
  • University Hills

    • Hill Side village is a small neighborhood surrounded by El Sereno to the north and east. University Hills borders it to the east. East Los Angeles and Boyle Heights surround it from the south, and Lincoln Heights to from west
